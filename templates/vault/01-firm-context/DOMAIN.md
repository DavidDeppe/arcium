## PoC Evaluation Criteria

When assessing whether a PoC is worth pursuing, apply these lenses:

1. **Time savings**: Does it meaningfully reduce manual effort on a known pain point?
2. **Accuracy**: Does it reduce error rates in important workflows?
3. **Impact**: Does it improve outcomes for clients or internal teams?
4. **Regulatory fit**: Can it be deployed without triggering additional compliance obligations?
5. **Integration feasibility**: Can it connect to existing systems without major IT investment?
6. **Explainability**: Can the AI's outputs be explained to stakeholders or auditors?
